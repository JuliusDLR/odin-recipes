TURKEY BACON,EGG & CHEESE

Ingredients

  • Turkey Bacon
  • 2 Whole Eggs
  • Cheddar Cheese

Preparation

  1. Heat up a pan over medium heat, once hot use cooking spray and add turkey bacon.
  2. Cook bacon for around 5 mins then flip and repeat until browned & crispy.
  3. Put bacon on paper towels then add 2 eggs to the pan and cook until somewhat solid.
  4. Once slightly solid add your cheese on top of it and allow it to melt at the same time your eggs are cooking.
  5. Here you have 2 options, OPTION 1) Either toast bread in a toaster or OPTION 2) toast your bread in that pan we been using. For this recipe we'll assume its the latter.
  6. Throw a slice of bread in the pan and move your eggs & cheese onto the slice.
  7. Then add the bacon as well as any sauces you want and finish with the other slice.
  8. Squish the sandwich and flip until bread is properly toasted.
  9. Cut your sandwich anyway you like and enjoy!
