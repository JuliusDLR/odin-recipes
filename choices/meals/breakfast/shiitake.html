SHIITAKE SCRAMBLE

Ingredients

  • 5 Shiitake Mushrooms
  • 3 Whole Eggs
  • 1 Lime

Preparation

  1. Chop shiitake mushrooms into bite sized pieces(Doesnt matter the size just make sure its 5 shrooms and chop em up)
  2. Whisk eggs in a bowl and add some salt, pepper & lime juice from 1 whole lime.
  3. Using cooking spray or any oil, oil the pan & add the mushrooms in.
  4. Once the mushrooms have browned, add your eggs & scramble until done.
  5. Enjoy!
