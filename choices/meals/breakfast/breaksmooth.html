BREAKFAST SMOOTHIE

Ingredients

  • 1 Scoop Vanilla Protein Powder
  • 1 cup Unsweetened Almond Mil
  • 1/4 cup Rolled Oats
  • 1/2 tsp Cinnamon Sugar
  • 1/2 tsp Maca Powder
  • Ice Cubes

Preparation

  1. Add all ingredients into a blender & blend until smooth.
  2. If too thick add more almond milk until desired consistency.
  3. Enjoy.
