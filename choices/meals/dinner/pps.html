PROTEIN PACKER SMOOTHIE

Ingredients

  • 8oz Almond Milk(Youll probably need more)
  • 2 Scoops of Protein Powder
  • 2 tsp chia seed
  • (OPTIONAL) Fruit Chunks

Preparation

  1. Add all the ingredients to blender and blend.
  2. For an addition of flavor feel free to add your fruit of choice.
  3. Enjoy!
