QUISQUEYAYITI

Ingredients

  • 8 Whole Wings
  • 3 cup White Rice
  • 1 can Green Pigeon Peas

Preparation

  1. Once clean, season the wings with garlic powder, onion powder, parsley & lemon pepper.
  2. Roast in air fryer at 275Â° for 50 mins.
  3. Put the rice in the rice cooker and select white rice.
  4. Blend half the can of peas with some water.
  5. Heat med-large pot on med-high heat & add olive oil.
  6. Add diced onions & peppers and saute.
  7. Add minced garlic & chicken bouillion cube and then mix.
  8. Add mashed and regular peas, saute for 2 mins.
  9. Add water, dominican oregano, tomato paste and auyama and mix.
  10. Simmer for 20 mins until it thickens and add more water if necessary.
  11. Turn heat off and let it cool
  12. Once cool, pour it into blender and blend once more. You may need to do multiple blends to be able to blend it all
  13. Once all has been blended return to pot and put it on medium-low heat. Taste test.
  14. Let rest for 10 mins before serving
  15. Plate your rice first, then the wings on the side and the pigeon peas over the rice.
  16. Enjoy!
