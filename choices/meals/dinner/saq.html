Ingredients

  • Pre-Seasoned Bourbon Marinated Salmon
  • 1 cup Asparagus
  • 1/2 cup Quinoa

Preparation

  1. Heat up a pan with olive oil on low heat.
  2. Add salmon start with skin up, cook each side for 5 mins each until done.
  3. Season Asparagus with garlic powder, onion powder & lemon pepper
  4. Roast Asparagus in air fryer at 290Â° for 25mins.
  5. Put quinoa in rice cooker and use white rice setting.
  6. Plate and Enjoy!
