TUNA & CRACKERS

Ingredients

  • 2 cans of Tuna
  • Mayonnaise
  • Ketchup
  • Franks Red Hot Sauce
  • Lime
  • Bunch of Crackers

Preparation

  1. Open and drain water from tuna cans then pour tuna in a bowl.
  2. Add 2tbsp scoops of mayo then add ketchup and hot sauce at your discretion. The consistency should be spreadable.
  3. Add crackers around edge of bowl until you have no space left(With small gaps that expose the tuna)
  4. Squeeze a lime onto the bowl covering all the tuna & crackers.
  5. Put in microwave for 1:17 minutes on high.
  6. Enjoy!
