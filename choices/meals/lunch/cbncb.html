CHICKEN BREAST & CHEESY BROCCOLI

Ingredients

  • 3 Sliced Chicken Breast or 1 Whole Chicken Breast
  • Birds Eye Cheesy Broccoli

Preparation

  1. Chop your chicken into bite sized pieces then clean them.
  2. Once clean season with garlic powder, onion powder, parsley and lemon pepper seasoning.
  3. Throw that in the air fryer at 275Â°F for 45 mins. Checking & flipping every 15 mins.
  4. The chicken may need more time depending on how crispy youd like it.
  5. Put the birds eye cheesy broccoli in microwave for 5 mins.
  6. There are 2 plating options, 1) Plate on an actual plate or 2)Cut the broccoli bag on its side & put the chicken in the bag, now carefully shake the bag & grab a fork and eat out the bag.
  7. Enjoy!
